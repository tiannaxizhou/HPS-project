Instructions

  • Players take turns to place some number of circles of different radius on the board. Your mission is to draw circles in the remain blank space.
  • Round 1: PlayerA starts to draw one circle with control of the center and radius of the circle. After playerA properly chooses a center and a point supposedly on the circle, a new circle will be drawn on the canvas. PlayerB then starts to repeat what playerA did in the remain blank space with one new circle just drawn.
  • Round 2: PlayerB first starts to draw a circle. Then PlayerA gets to draw.
  • Repeat turns.

To Play

  • If the new drawn circle somehow steps on the area of other previous circles or is beyond the border of the canvas, then the circle will be automatically narrowed down to proper radius to fit in a blank space of the canvas.
